Who we are

Malta's go-to

InstaStore is a locally owned mini market serving the community of Malta. Whether you need a cold drink at midnight, fresh produce in the morning, or household essentials on your way home — we're always here.

We stock over 1,000 products across groceries, snacks, drinks, dairy, and household goods, with new stock arriving daily.
